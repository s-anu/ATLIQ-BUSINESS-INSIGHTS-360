
# Business Insights 360

This Power BI project was designed to address the business needs of AtliQ Hardware, a company operating in both e-commerce and retail sectors. By analyzing over 1 million data entries across finance, sales, marketing, and supply chain domains, the project delivers comprehensive insights that support informed decisions and drive strategic business growth

## Finance View

Purpose:

This page focuses on customer and product sales performance. It allows the sales team to evaluate how different customers, regions, and product segments contribute to overall sales and profitability.

It helps answer questions like:

* Who are our top-performing customers?

* Which product segments are driving revenue?

* How efficient are we in terms of unit economics?

<img width="1290" height="722" alt="atq_2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a9e12a71-4ec5-4f0e-8a1a-32db16a3c911" />


Key Components:

* Customer Performance
Provides a clear view of high-value vs low-value customers

* Performance Matrix

Great for customer segmentation and strategy building.

* Product Performance

Identifies which product lines are driving sales and profit.

* Unit Economics 
Provides a quick view of sales deductions and profitability structure.

Business Value:

Helps sales leaders track performance by customer and product segment.

Identifies high-revenue but low-margin customers to improve pricing/discounting strategy.

Supports product strategy by showing which categories contribute most to revenue and profit.

Improves decision-making for account managers, sales directors, and executives.

## Supplychain View

Purpose:

This page monitors forecast accuracy, net error, and supply risks across customers and products. It helps supply chain managers optimize demand forecasting, inventory management, and risk assessment.

<img width="1286" height="717" alt="atq_5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c2e4dd82-963c-4bf1-b969-3d19c5a948d3" />


Key Components:

* KPI Tiles

Forecast Accuracy %, Net Error, and Absolute Error

These KPIs track forecast quality and deviation trends.

* Key Metrics by Customer 

Tracks Forecast Accuracy %, Net Error, Risk Type (OOS / EI) for each customer.

Helps identify which customers drive major supply chain risks.

* Accuracy / Net Error Trend 

Shows accuracy fluctuations and error spikes 

Useful for seasonality analysis and for improving forecasting.

* Key Metrics by Products 

Evaluates Forecast Accuracy, Net Error, and Risk across product segments.

Identifies product-level supply chain risks.

 Business Value:

Improves forecast reliability by pinpointing weak areas.

Identifies customers/products with high demand volatility.

Differentiates between OOS risks (lost sales) and EI risks (overstock costs).

Supports inventory optimization, demand planning, and supply chain resilience.
## Executive View

Purpose:

This page provides a high-level overview for executives and leadership, combining financial performance, market share, customer insights, and risk indicators. It enables data-driven strategic decisions across revenue growth, profitability, and competitive positioning.

 Key Components:

* KPI Tiles
  
Gives executives a snapshot of overall company health.

* Key Insights by Sub Zone 

Highlights regional strengths and risks 

* PC Market Share Trend – AtliQ vs Competitors 

 Helps executives assess competitive positioning.

* Yearly Trend by Revenue, GM%, Net Profit %, Market Share 

 Identifies long-term strategic challenges.

* Revenue by Division & Channel 

 Shows where revenue is concentrated and which sales channels drive growth.

* Top 5 Customers & Products by Revenue 
    
Focuses executives on key customers/products driving the majority of sales.

 Business Value:

Provides end-to-end visibility across regions, products, customers, and competitors.

Helps leadership balance growth vs profitability.

Identifies high-revenue regions/customers but also highlights risks (OOS/EI).

Tracks competitive market share trends → informs long-term strategy.
